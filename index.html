Just Diagnosed?
Take a Breath.

Receiving an HIV diagnosis can be overwhelming, but you are not alone. Modern medicine means you can live a long, healthy life. We are here to guide you through the first steps.

  • It's manageable. HIV is a chronic manageable condition, like diabetes.
  • Care is free. Government ART clinics across Pakistan provide free medication.
  • Confidentiality is key. Your status is protected by law.
