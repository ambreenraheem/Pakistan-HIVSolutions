Advocacy & Rights

We stand for the rights of every Pakistani living with HIV. Learn about legal protections, awareness campaigns, and how to fight discrimination.

Your Legal Protections

Pakistan has legal frameworks at both national and provincial levels that protect people living with HIV (PLHIV) from discrimination. Knowing these laws is the key to defending your rights.

Right to Healthcare

No hospital or clinic can refuse treatment based on your HIV status.

Right to Employment

Disclosure of HIV status is not required for employment, and termination based on status is illegal.

Right to Privacy

Mandatory HIV testing without consent is prohibited in most contexts.
